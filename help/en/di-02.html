INDUSTRIAL DISTRICT

  • Benefits Each Level
  • Maximum Benefits

The facilities in the Industrial District allow for passive production of the 4 basic resources ‒ Food, Petroleum, Alloys and Composites over time, but also for their permanent protection against offensive actions. You can check up on your current economy status from this District as well.
